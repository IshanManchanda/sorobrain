Competitions

Participate in online competitions with your friends and win exciting prizes!

How it Works
1. Register for a competition you're interested in.
2. Participate in the competition by giving online quizzes!
3. Increase your chances of victory with practice quizzes and workshops!
4. Submit your responses before the deadline.
5. Get a widely recognized certificate of merit or participation!
Information for Schools and Organizations
Registering for your students: 
1. Select the competition you want to register your students.
2. Select "school register" and fill out the details.
3. Once you complete the payment you will get a list of activation codes. 
4. Give one code to each student and ask them to go to the competition page and select the 'register with code' option.
5. With the codes you will also get a directions guide.
