OUR TEAM

Seema Jain

Founder, Sorobrain

Seema Jain

With more than two decades of experience and having passion for the French language, Seema enjoys the challenge of training people in a digital world. As a founder & CEO, she practices a Laissez-faire management style and believes strongly in transparency, both internally and externally.

Gaurika Kapoor

Digital Marketing and Communications Manager

Gaurika Kapoor

Gaurika is eSorobrain's Digital Marketing and Communications Manager. She is detail- oriented and manages our interactions with learners.
Gaurika has a myriad of interests, she is an avid reader and enjoys baking and writing. She is also learning French and has achieved the A2 level in the DELF examination.

Param Kapur

Full Stack Developer

Param Kapur

Param is a full stack web developer with more than 4 years of experience. Currently he is a Freshman at Reed College in Portland, OR. He built Sorobrain using Django on the backend, with a PostgreSQL database, and Amazon S3 for media. Check out his Github for more details!
