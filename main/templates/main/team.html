OUR TEAM

Seema Jain

Founder, Sorobrain


Seema Jain

With more than two decades of experience and having passion for the French language, Seema enjoys the challenge of training people in a digital world. As a founder & CEO, she practices a Laissez-faire management style and believes strongly in transparency, both internally and externally.

Gaurika Kapoor

Marketing Consultant


Gaurika Kapoor

Gaurika is Sorobrain's Marketing Advisor. With her expertise in the field, she assists in developing strategies and goals for the academy and helps in keeping up with ongoing trends. Gaurika has a myriad of interests, she is an avid reader and enjoys baking and writing. She is going to pursue her Masters at King’s College, University of London and plans to appear for B1 Level in French by the end of 2021 while DELF A2 already achieved.

Naisha Arora

Digital Marketing and Communications Manager

Naisha Arora

Naisha is Sorobrain’s marketing and outreach incharge. She excels at strategic planning, project implementation and resource allocation. She loves to connect with new people every day and is always ready to help! In her free time, she likes to watch shows, read and surf social media. Naisha is currently pursuing French at A2 Level for DELF examination.

Param Kapur
