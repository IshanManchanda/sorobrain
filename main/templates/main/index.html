About Sorobrain

Since 2009, Sorobrain® has specialized in providing a fun and active learning environment for its French language courses through activities like theatrical performances,
karaoke, quizzes & competitions, games, puzzles, etc. where the knowledge is put into action. Our motive is to make students absorb the language. As digital technology continues to evolve, e_Sorobrain® is the digital transformation of Sorobrain® that aims at providing the same at the comfort of one’s place while extending its services to the entire world.

“Learning the fun way, is the culture established & promoted at Sorobrain®."
-Seema Jain [Founder]
